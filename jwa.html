About the Author

Hi there! I’m James Dominic Liao. I’m currently unemployed, but a believer in God, and I love writing books, stories, and scenarios.

Over the years, I’ve experimented with many writing styles and genres. I’m a self-published author, sharing my creations online for anyone to enjoy.

While I mainly write science fiction and fantasy, I also explore other genres. My stories aim to entertain, inspire, and sometimes provoke thought.
