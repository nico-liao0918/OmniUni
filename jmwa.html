About the Author

Hi there! I’m James Dominic Liao. I’m currently unemployed but a believer in God, and I love writing books, stories, and scenarios.

Over the years, I’ve written in many different styles and genres. I’m a self-published author and writer, sharing my creations online for everyone to enjoy.

While I mainly write science fiction and fantasy, I also explore other genres. My stories are designed to entertain, inspire, and sometimes even provoke thought.
